# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines

## Brand Personality
* Authoritative — speak with confidence and expertise
* Trustworthy — clients are vulnerable, make them feel safe
* Direct — lawyers are precise, copy should be too
* Empathetic — understand what clients are going through
* Results-driven — always lead with outcomes and wins

## Grammar & Mechanics
* **Headlines:** Powerful and direct. "We Fight For You." not "We Try Our Best"
* **Body copy:** Clear, jargon-free, empathetic. Speak to the person not the case.
* **CTAs:** Action-oriented. "Get Free Consultation" not "Contact Us"
* **Avoid:** Legal jargon in marketing copy, passive voice, vague promises
* **Use:** Specific win rates, case results, years of experience, real outcomes

## Market Context
* This is a UNIVERSAL template — no specific city, state or country references
* Copy works for any law firm anywhere in the world
* Practice areas cover: Personal Injury, Criminal Defense, Corporate Law, Family Law, Immigration

## Terminology Guide

| Do Not Use | Use Instead |
| :--- | :--- |
| "We try to help" | "We fight for you" |
| "Contact us" | "Get your free consultation" |
| "Legal services" | "Powerful legal representation" |
| "We handle cases" | "We win cases" |
| "Affordable rates" | "Transparent pricing" |
| Any specific location | Remove entirely |

## Copy Examples by Section

**Hero headline style:**
"Fighting For What Matters Most." — emotional, powerful, direct

**Practice area style:**
"Personal Injury — When someone else's negligence changes your life, 
we fight to make it right."
Lead with the client's situation, then the solution.

**Stats style:**
"$50M+ recovered for clients" — dollar amounts first, most impactful
"95% success rate" — percentage, clean

**Testimonial style:**
"Vanguard Law got me the settlement I deserved. 
They fought for me when no one else would."
— Emotion first, outcome second

**CTA style:**
"Get Your Free Consultation →" — specific, no-risk, clear next step
